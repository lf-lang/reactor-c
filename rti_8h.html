typedef struct federate_t federate_t

Information about a federate known to the RTI, including its runtime state, mode of execution, and connectivity with other federates. The list of upstream and downstream federates does not include those that are connected via a "physical" connection (one denoted with ~>) because those connections do not impose any scheduling constraints.
